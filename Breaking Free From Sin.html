Breaking Free From Sin

(Romans 6:5-23 NKJV) "For if we have been united together in the likeness of His death, certainly we also shall be in the likeness of His resurrection..."

We have the power to no longer live in sin, having been freed through Christ’s death and resurrection. The body of sin is done away with, and we are no longer slaves to sin, but alive to God in Christ Jesus.

The Struggle with Sin:

The Bible teaches that Christians have victory over both the penalty and the power of sin. However, we still struggle with sin in our daily lives. It is important to recognize that we are free from sin in our true eternal selves, and this can help us live victoriously.

The Solution to Sin:

The answer is to walk in the Spirit, as the flesh and the Spirit are in conflict. By filling our lives with the things of the Spirit—through prayer, Bible study, and fellowship with others—we overcome the desires of the flesh and live according to God’s will.

Prayers for Strength:

  • Grace - For God's kindness and the power to resist sin.
  • Mercy - For deliverance from the messes we find ourselves in.
  • Peace - For God's blessings to integrate our lives and prevent sin.
  • Understanding - To know what God has done for us and the power we have over sin.
  • Wisdom & Discernment - To apply our understanding to real-life situations.
  • Love of God - To be filled with His love and make spiritual thinking easy.
  • To Be Filled With The Spirit - For boldness, joy, and perception of God's ways.
