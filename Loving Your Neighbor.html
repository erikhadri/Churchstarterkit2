Love of God and love of neighbor are the two great commandments...

Step Two

Step Three

Christian Compassion Priorities

May I suggest an order of priorities for Christian compassion:

1. Our spouse.
2. Our children.
3. Our aged parents.
4. The church.
5. The basic needs of the world.
