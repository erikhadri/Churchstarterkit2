What Has Happened To Me?

From Darkness To Light

(Ephesians 2:1-10 NKJV) And you He made alive, who were dead in trespasses and sins... For by grace you have been saved through faith, and that not of yourselves; it is the gift of God.

The Big Change

There has been a huge change in your life. You have moved from being "dead in trespasses and sins" to being "alive together with Christ". God's life, which is "eternal life" has been put into your heart. God has come to live in you and be at home in your life...

Freedom From Control

Before becoming a Christian, you were controlled by "the prince of the power of the air" - the Devil. He had influence over your life, but now, through Christ, his control is broken...

God's Purpose For You

God saves you for a purpose - to live a holy and responsible life. "For we are His workmanship, created in Christ Jesus for good works, which God prepared beforehand that we should walk in them" (Ephesians 2:10)...

Living As A New Creation

Your transformation means you must actively put your new life into practice every day. Walk in faith, love, and obedience to Christ, knowing that He has a plan for you...
