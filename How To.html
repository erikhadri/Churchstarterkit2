A Seven Step Process

  • Acknowledge your sin before God.
  • Acknowledge the sin's grip on you and your desire to be free.
  • Ask for God's forgiveness.
  • Ask Jesus to fill you with His Holy Spirit.
  • Make restitution and reconcile with those you may have hurt.
  • Destroy any objects involved with your sin.
  • Press on to higher ground through obedience, prayer, fellowship, and Bible study.
