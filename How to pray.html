How To Pray

(Matthew 6:5-18 NKJV) "And when you pray, you shall not be like the hypocrites..."

The Lord's Prayer gives us essential guidance on how to pray sincerely and effectively. It emphasizes a personal, heartfelt connection with God, rather than praying for public recognition.

Basic truths about prayer:

  • Pray believing that God hears you, even in secret.
  • Pray for God's honor, His Kingdom, and for righteousness on Earth.

Where, When, and How to Pray:

(Luke 5:16 NKJV) "So He Himself often withdrew into the wilderness and prayed."

Jesus prayed alone, in a quiet place, with deep emotion and sincerity. This serves as a powerful model for our own prayer lives.
